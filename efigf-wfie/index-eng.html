Wide Format Imaging Equipment

National Master Standing Offer (NMSO)
EZ107-070002/001/VAN to EZ107-070002/009/VAN
Wide Format Imaging Equipment
NMSO Period: July 15, 2010 - July 12, 2012

These National Master Standing Offers (NMSOs) include categories of equipment that are intended to satisfy the majority of the Government of Canada's wide format imaging equipment needs. These NMSOs are for the supply, delivery, installation, and service of Wide Format Imaging Equipment for Graphics and Engineering Applications, including accessories and supplies on an "as and when requested" basis to locations throughout Canada, excluding Comprehensive Land Claims Areas.

This series of standing offers will be in place for up to three years and is a mandatory usage method of supply. This means that all Wide Format Imaging Equipment must be purchased using these standing offers, unless an exception is approved by the Standing Offer Authority.

The Products available for purchase under this Standing Offer are organized into Categories and Sub-Categories. The Products each Offeror is authorized to supply under this Standing Offer including their applicable prices are set out in the Best Value Grid product and pricing details (www).
